About American Skyline Quality Standards

Your trusted global partner for ISO certification and compliance solutions

Who We Are

The American Skyline Quality Standards (ASQS) is a worldwide association of accreditation bodies and other bodies interested in conformity assessment in the fields of management systems, products, processes, services, personnel, validation and verification and other similar programmes of conformity assessment.

Our primary function is to develop a single worldwide program of conformity assessment which reduces risk for businesses and their customers by assuring them that accredited certificates and validation and verification statements may be relied upon.

American Skyline Quality Standards & Services (ASQS) is one of the leading certificates provider. We are backed up by a highly professional and qualified team, we have been certifying business enterprises across various sectors of economies for their management systems by assuring that they meet the requirements of International Organization for Standardization (ISO).

Quality Management Certification

A QMS ensures that an organization can consistently deliver products or services that meet customer and regulatory requirements, and that it continually improves its effectiveness and efficiency.

Environment Management System

An EMS is a set of processes and practices that enable an organization to reduce its environmental impacts, increase efficiency, and achieve environmental goals.

Health & Safety Certification

A Health and Safety Management System is a set of policies, procedures, and practices designed to prevent workplace accidents, injuries, illnesses, and to ensure compliance with health and safety regulations.

Information Security & IT Certification

An Information Security and IT Management System is a structured framework used by organizations to manage and protect their digital information and technology assets.

Compliance & Risk Certification

A compliance certificate confirms that an entity (individual or organization) meets specific regulatory standards, legal requirements, or industry-specific rules. Compliance and Risk Certification refers to formal recognition that an organization, system, or individual adheres to established laws, standards, or best practices for managing compliance and risks.

Specialty Standards Certification

A speciality standard certificate refers to a certification issued for a specific industry, product, process, or area of expertise that goes beyond general ISO or compliance certifications. These certificates are designed to address specialized requirements in certain sectors and are often industry-specific or application-specific.
